# Product Notes

AI Life Assistant is one focused product with three practical sections.

The goal is to help users with everyday tasks, career progress, and meal planning without making them write long prompts from scratch.

## Audience

- Students
- Job seekers
- Freelancers
- Home users
- Small business owners

## Product principles

- Keep the first screen simple.
- Build small daily-use features first.
- Make the app mobile-friendly.
- Prefer clear flows over too many options.
- Save complex features for later.

## MVP sections

- Daily Life Assistant
- Job and Freelance Coach
- Meal and Budget Planner
